Built by a developer who couldn't market his own app.

Faze started with a simple frustration. I shipped an app I was proud of — and then watched it sit at zero downloads. I cold-DMed creators and got ignored. The few who replied wanted four figures up front, with no promise it would work. I'm a builder, not a marketer, and the whole thing felt rigged against people like me.

So I built the tool I wished existed: a marketplace where developers post a campaign and real creators come to them, where money sits safely in escrow, and where you only pay when a deal actually closes. That's Faze — made for vibe coders who'd rather be building than chasing influencers.
